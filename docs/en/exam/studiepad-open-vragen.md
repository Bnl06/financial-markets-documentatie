# Open-question study path

## 2-day emergency plan

### Day 1 — core mechanisms and formulas

- Unit 1: actors, balance sheets, direct/semi-direct/indirect finance.
- Unit 2: Fisher, simple interest, compounding, PV/FV, bond pricing, YTM.
- Units 3-4: repo, ratings, covenants, orders, indices, DDM.
- Complete Practice exam 1.

### Day 2 — risk, banking, crisis and regulation

- Units 5-7: portfolio risk, forwards/futures, options payoff/profit/break-even.
- Units 9-12: intermediation, banking, 2008 crisis, regulation/supervision.
- Complete and correct Practice exam 2 or 3.
