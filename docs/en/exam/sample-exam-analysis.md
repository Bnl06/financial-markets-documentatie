!!! warning "Check with the official course documents"
    In the sample excerpt, the correlation in question 13 appears as `-0.50%`. In finance exercises this often means `-0.50` as a correlation coefficient. Check the official version or instructions before copying the exact number.

## Main conclusions

- The examiner often asks for **one exact detail**: BBB, Single B, market order, stop-loss, arranger.
- Calculations are short but require choosing the right formula without a formula sheet.
- Multiple-response questions require discipline: judge every option separately.
- Banking, crisis and regulation terms appear not only as theory, but also in applied questions.

!!! tip "Best way to practise"
    First complete the practice exams without answers. Then correct them with the model answers and label each error as: **term not known**, **formula error**, **read too fast** or **missing link**.
