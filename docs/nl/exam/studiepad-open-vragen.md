# Studiepad open vragen

## 2-daags noodplan

### Dag 1 — kernmechanismes en formules

- Unit 1: actoren, balans, direct/semi-direct/indirect finance.
- Unit 2: Fisher, simple interest, compounding, PV/FV, bond pricing, YTM.
- Unit 3-4: repo, ratings, covenants, orders, indices, DDM.
- Oefenexamen 1 maken.

### Dag 2 — risico, banking, crisis en regulation

- Unit 5-7: portfolio risk, forwards/futures, options payoff/profit/break-even.
- Unit 9-12: intermediation, banking, 2008 crisis, regulation/supervision.
- Oefenexamen 2 of 3 maken en verbeteren.

1. Begrippen met hoge prioriteit.
2. Rekenformules zonder formuleblad.
3. Voorbeeldexamenanalyse.
4. Banking/crisis/regulation verbanden.
