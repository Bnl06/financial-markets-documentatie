# Oefenexamen 1

!!! info "Examenmodus"
    Maak dit zonder formuleblad. Reken met Excel indien nodig en schrijf bij open vragen in kernzinnen.

Puntenverdeling: 20 punten. MC = 1 punt, begripsvragen = 1-2 punten, berekeningen/open vragen = 2-3 punten.

### 1. MC

### 2. Begrip

### 3. Berekening

### 4. Open vraag

Compare direct, semi-direct and indirect finance.

### 5. MC meerdere antwoorden

Which tools support a safe and sound banking system? deposit insurance / capital adequacy / minimum liquidity / recovery plans

### 6. Berekening

Compute FV of €30,000 for 12 years at 2.5% with quarterly compounding.

### 7. Begrip

Explain why Single B bonds are not investment grade.

### 8. Open vraag

Explain two alternatives to auctions for government bond issuance.

### 9. Berekening

Two-asset portfolio: weights 75/25, vols 15% and 7%, correlation -0.50. Compute volatility.

### 10. Film/casus

### 11. Begrip

Define covenant and negative pledge.

### 12. Open vraag
