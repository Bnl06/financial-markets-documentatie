# Panic — The Untold Story of the 2008 Financial Crisis

!!! abstract "Waarom relevant voor het examen?"
    De film is relevant als casus om de crisismechanismen te kunnen uitleggen: vertrouwen, bank runs, leverage, securitisation, systemic risk, too big to fail, bail-outs en regulation.

- Welke instellingen waren systemically important?
- Waarom was vertrouwen belangrijker dan alleen boekhoudkundige cijfers?
- Wat is de link tussen securitisation, MBS/CDO/ABS en onduidelijk risico?
- Waarom twijfelden beleidsmakers tussen bail-out en no bail-out?
- Welke lessen leiden naar strengere capital, liquidity en recovery plan requirements?

## Modelantwoord filmvraag

**Vraag:** Explain one reason why policymakers feared systemic risk in 2008.

**Antwoord:** Policymakers feared that the failure of one large interconnected institution could trigger losses and panic elsewhere. Banks and shadow banks were linked through funding markets, securitised products and confidence. Therefore, one failure could create contagion and threaten the payment and credit system.
